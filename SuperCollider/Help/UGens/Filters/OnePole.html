OnePole.ar(in, coef, mul, add)


A one pole filter. Implements the formula :


out(i) = ((1 - abs(coef)) * in(i)) + (coef * out(i-1))


in - input signal to be processed

coef - feedback coefficient. Should be between -1 and +1
