LFNoise1 ramp noise


LFNoise1.ar(freq, mul, add)

LFNoise1.kr(freq, mul, add)


Generates linearly interpolated random values at a rate. 

freq - approximate rate at which to generate random values.


see also: LFDNoise1, LFNoise0, LFNoise2
